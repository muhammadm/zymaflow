Sustainability & ESG

Built to Reduce.
Designed to Replace.

ZymaFlow was designed around circular economy principles from the ground up. By redirecting organic-rich co-product streams that would otherwise be discarded, our platform creates measurable environmental value across waste diversion, carbon displacement, and resource efficiency simultaneously.

Organic Waste Diversion

Co-product streams diverted from wastewater and landfill pathways reduce biological oxygen demand (BOD) loading on municipal treatment infrastructure — measurable, auditable, and directly tied to feedstock volume processed.

Waste Reduction
Low-Carbon Protein

Fermentation-derived protein carries a fraction of the carbon footprint of animal-derived equivalents — no land use change, no feed conversion losses, no enteric methane.

Carbon Displacement
Ingredient Displacement

Each product stream displaces a higher-footprint incumbent — animal-derived protein, synthetically produced nutraceutical actives, and rendered animal meal in pet nutrition.

Circular Inputs
Industrial Symbiosis

One industry's co-product stream becomes another's premium input — with low energy requirements and no virgin resource extraction. Applicable across fermentation sectors: brewing, dairy processing, distilling, and industrial fermentation.

Fermentation industries generate substantial volumes of co-product material that are routinely undervalued. A new class of biorefinery technology is changing that equation.

Our Platform

Proprietary Biorefinery
Technology

A proprietary biorefinery platform that converts fermentation co-product streams into multiple distinct, market-ready ingredient categories. Patent pending. Designed from the outset for collaborative, consortium-based deployment.

01

Canadian Ingredient Supply Chain

Canada imports the majority of the premium nutraceutical and functional food ingredients it consumes. ZymaFlow's platform recovers these ingredient categories domestically — from Canadian fermentation industry co-product streams — reducing import dependence and establishing a new domestic supply chain in an underserved value chain segment.

02

Multi-Stream Valorization

A single co-product input yields multiple distinct, market-ready ingredient categories — each with preserved native functional properties. This multi-stream recovery model maximises the economic value captured per kilogram of feedstock, delivering commercial outcomes that single-product approaches cannot replicate.

03

Built for Consortium Deployment

The platform architecture is designed for collaborative deployment with fermentation industry feedstock partners, ingredient offtakers, and food manufacturers. Modular and scalable, it is suited to consortium-based co-investment from pilot through industrial scale — reducing capital risk for all partners.
